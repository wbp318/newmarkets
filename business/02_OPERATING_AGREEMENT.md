### ARTICLE V — PROFITS, LOSSES, AND DISTRIBUTIONS

**5.1 Allocation.** Profits and losses shall be allocated to the Members in proportion to their membership interests.

**5.2 Distributions.** Distributions shall be made at such times and in such amounts as determined by the Managing Member, in proportion to membership interests. The Company shall retain sufficient funds for operations before making distributions.

**5.3 Tax Treatment.** As a single-member LLC, the Company shall be treated as a disregarded entity for federal income tax purposes unless the Member elects otherwise (e.g., S-Corp election via IRS Form 2553).

---

### ARTICLE VI — BOOKS, RECORDS, AND ACCOUNTING

**6.1 Books and Records.** The Company shall maintain complete and accurate books and records of all transactions at the principal place of business.

**6.2 Fiscal Year.** The fiscal year of the Company shall be the calendar year (January 1 — December 31).

**6.3 Bank Accounts.** All Company funds shall be deposited in a bank account in the name of the Company. No Company funds shall be commingled with personal funds of any Member.

---

### ARTICLE VII — TRANSFER OF MEMBERSHIP INTERESTS

**7.1 Restrictions.** No Member may transfer, sell, or assign their membership interest without the written consent of all other Members.

**7.2 Right of First Refusal.** If a Member wishes to sell their interest, the remaining Members shall have the right of first refusal to purchase the interest at fair market value.

---

### ARTICLE VIII — DISSOLUTION

**8.1 Events of Dissolution.** The Company shall be dissolved upon:
- The written consent of Members holding a majority of membership interests
- The entry of a judicial decree of dissolution
- Any event that makes it unlawful for the Company to continue

**8.2 Winding Up.** Upon dissolution, the Managing Member shall wind up the Company's affairs, pay all debts and obligations, and distribute remaining assets to Members in proportion to their membership interests.

---

### ARTICLE IX — LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

**9.1 Limited Liability.** No Member shall be personally liable for the debts, obligations, or liabilities of the Company solely by reason of being a Member.

**9.2 Indemnification.** The Company shall indemnify each Member against any losses, claims, or expenses arising from their authorized actions on behalf of the Company, except in cases of willful misconduct or gross negligence.

---

### ARTICLE X — AMENDMENTS

This Agreement may be amended only by the written consent of Members holding a majority of the membership interests.
