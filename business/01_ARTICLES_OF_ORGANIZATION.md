# Articles of Organization — 318ecom LLC

File with the Louisiana Secretary of State. You can file online or by mail.

**Online:** https://www.sos.la.gov/BusinessServices/FileBusinessDocuments/Pages/default.aspx
**Mail:** Secretary of State, P.O. Box 94125, Baton Rouge, LA 70804-9125
**Fee:** $100 online / $75 mail (plus $25 initial report)

---

## Information You Will Need

When you go to file online, the form will ask for the following. Have this ready:

### 1. LLC Name
```
318ecom LLC
```

### 2. Purpose
```
To engage in any and all lawful business activities for which a limited
liability company may be organized under the Louisiana Limited Liability
Company Law, including but not limited to e-commerce, international trade,
digital marketing, and product sourcing.
```

### 3. Duration
```
Perpetual
```
(This means the LLC exists until you decide to dissolve it.)

### 4. Registered Agent
The registered agent is the person who receives legal documents on behalf of the LLC. This can be you.

```
Name:       [YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME]
Address:    [YOUR PHYSICAL STREET ADDRESS — not a P.O. Box]
City:       Lake Providence (or your actual city)
State:      Louisiana
ZIP:        71254 (or your actual ZIP)
Parish:     East Carroll
```

**Note:** The registered agent address must be a physical Louisiana street address, not a P.O. Box.

### 5. Principal Business Office
```
Address:    [YOUR PHYSICAL STREET ADDRESS]
City:       [YOUR CITY]
State:      Louisiana
ZIP:        [YOUR ZIP]
Parish:     East Carroll
```

### 6. Organizer
The person filing the articles. This is you.
```
Name:       [YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME]
Address:    [YOUR ADDRESS]
```

### 7. Manager or Member-Managed
Choose one:
- **Member-managed** — all members (owners) run the business (choose this if it's just you)
- **Manager-managed** — designated managers run it, members are passive investors

**For a single-owner LLC, choose Member-Managed.**

### 8. Initial Members/Managers
```
Name:       [YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME]
Address:    [YOUR ADDRESS]
Title:      Member
```

### 9. Initial Report
Filed at the same time. It asks for the same info above plus:
- NAICS code: **454110** (Electronic Shopping and Mail-Order Houses)
- Estimated number of employees: 0 (or 1 if counting yourself)

---

## After Filing

1. You'll get a confirmation with your **File Number** — save this
2. The Secretary of State processes filings within 3-5 business days (online is faster)
3. Once approved, order a **certified copy** ($30) — you'll need it for your bank account
4. Your LLC is now officially formed — move on to the EIN application
